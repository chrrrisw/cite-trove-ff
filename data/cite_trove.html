Cite Trove How-To

This Firefox extension allows users of the Trove Newspaper site to copy selected OCR text to their system clipboard along with information such as the Newspaper Title, Issue and Page reference.

The format of the copied text can be configured through the preferences page for the addon.

To modify the format, use the following codes along with any other text of your choice.

  • %A - Persistent Article URL (preferred)
  • %U - Article URL
  • %u - Shortened Article URL
  • %C - Current date and time
  • %T - Newspaper Title
  • %I - Newspaper Issue
  • %P - Article Page number
  • %H - Article Headline
  • %Q - Selected OCR Text
  • %n - Start a New line

Example 1:

%T, %I, page %P

Would produce the something like:

The Canberra Times (ACT : 1926 - 1995), Thursday 3 July 1975, page 6

Example 2:

Title: %T%nIssue: %I, page %P%n[Quote start]%n%Q%n[Quote end]%n

Would produce the something like:

Title: The Canberra Times (ACT : 1926 - 1995)
Issue: Thursday 3 July 1975, page 6
[Quote start]
Space
MOSCOW, Wednesday (AAP). — The Soviet and US space agencies wound up preparations yesterday for this month's Soyuz-Apollo link-up in an atmosphere of "deep satisfaction and mutual understanding", according to Tass news agency.
[Quote end]

Original format:

The original format is: %A%n%C%n%T%n%I, page %P%n[Quote]%n%Q%n[Quote]%n

Which produces something like:

http://nla.gov.au/nla.news-article110644884
2013-06-11T01:10:55.553Z
The Canberra Times (ACT : 1926 - 1995)
Thursday 3 July 1975, page 6
[Quote]
Space
